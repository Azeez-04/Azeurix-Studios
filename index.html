Movie Summary

INSCRYPTION is an atmospheric animation by Azeez. Three friends explore an uncharted cave and stumble upon an enigmatic ATM promising a grand prize — only to those who enter the correct code. The hunt becomes a tense, inventive quest blending mystery, humor, and the unexpected dangers of curiosity. Secrets are uncovered, friendships tested, and each twist pushes the trio to rethink what they value most.
